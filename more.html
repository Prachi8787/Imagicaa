The Theme Park

Rides-At Imagica, there are many different rides which can be categorised into family rides (like Bump it Boats, Save the Pirate and Splash Ahoy!), kids rides (like Happy Wheels, Tubby Takes Off and Humpty Falls), and thrill rides (like Dare 2 Drop, Nitro and Scream Machine). Overall there are 26 rides and attractions at Imagica. There are also many themed attractions where you can enjoy, have fun and experience adventure. Some of such themed attractions are Alibaba Aur Chalis Chor, Motion Box Theatre, Mr India, Salilmgarh and Wrath of Gods.


Park Timings: 10:30am – 8:00pm
Ride Timings: 11:00am – 7:00pm

The Water Park

Water Rides-Aquamagica is a water park in Adlabs Imagica where you can enjoy many water rides. The rides and attractions at Aquamagica are Zip Zap Zoom, Loopy Woopy, Yell-O!, Splash, Raftaastic!, Swirl Whirl, Soakerz, The Pirate Bay, Floatsa, Boomeranggo, Twisty Turvy and Wacky Waves. Restaurants-Aquamagica also houses many good restaurants in its premises like Red Bonnet Express, Sunbeatz Bar, AMMOS- The Food Court and Blue Stone Cafe.


Park Timings: 10:30am – 6:00pm
Ride Timings: 11:00am – 5:00pm

The Snow Park

Adlabs Imagica’s Snow Park is India’s largest indoor snow park spreading over 15,000 sq.ft. Come and enjoy a unique experience with 100% pure and cool snow at this snow park. The major attractions of this snow park are Snow Games & experiences, pure, edible & real snow, Sleighs, toboggans & rafts.
